# Governance Glossary

**Alignment Reviewers**  
Experts applying the E-IP Alignment Protocol to ethical assessments.

**Semantic Stewards**  
Custodians of terminology, symbolic clarity, and semantic coherence.

**Maintainers**  
Technical stewards who review code, schemas, and protocol logic.

**Decision Log**  
Permanent, immutable record of all governance decisions.

**RFC (Request for Comment)**  
Optional long-form document for proposing substantial changes.

**EAR (Ethical Alignment Review)**  
Formal evaluation required for ethically impactful changes.

**Protocol Layers**  
E-IP is composed of Governance, Alignment, and Semantic layers.

**Cross-Layer Review**  
Simultaneous evaluation by technical, semantic, and alignment teams.

**Release**  
Versioned publication of updated specs or schemas.

**Safeguard**  
A constraint imposed to mitigate identified ethical risks.
